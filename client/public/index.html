About SafeStocker

SafeStocker helps small vendors manage inventory with accuracy and ease.

The platform supports both barcode scanning and barcode creation, allowing you to add products in seconds—no complicated setup required.

Once your items are added, SafeStocker continuously tracks stock levels, restock needs, and expiry dates. You receive early alerts for products that are about to expire, helping you prevent losses and maintain a healthy, reliable inventory.

SafeStocker also analyzes your sales patterns and product movement. Based on this, it provides smart suggestions for discounts—especially on slow-moving or soon-to-expire items—so you can clear stock on time and improve your profit margins.

Everything stays organized in one dashboard: product details, quantities, expiry alerts, barcodes, and discount insights. This saves time, reduces waste, and gives small vendors a clean, efficient way to manage their entire store with confidence.

How It Works

1. Add Products

Scan barcodes or manually enter product details including expiry dates. SafeStocker learns about your inventory instantly.

2. Track Stock

Monitor quantities, expiry dates, and restock levels all in one dashboard. Get automatic alerts before products expire.

3. Smart Discounts

Receive AI-powered discount suggestions for slow-moving items and soon-to-expire products to minimize waste.

4. Easy Billing

Use our integrated billing module to generate invoices and track sales seamlessly.
